# Lobster Bisque

## Fat
 - 9 TBSP Butter

## Meat
- 6 Tails

## Fluid
- 32oz Stock
- 2 Cup Heavy Cream
- 1/4 Cup Brandy
- 1 Cup White Wine
- 3 Cup Sherry

## Vegetables
- 1 Cup Carrot Finely Chopped (9 Carrtots)
- 1 Cup Celery (9 Stalks)
- 1 Cup Onion (2 Onions)
- 8 Cloves Garlic
- 8oz Tomato Paste

## Spices
- 9 tbsp Flour
- 3 tsp Paprika
- 1 tsp cayenne
- 6 bay leaves
